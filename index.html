Research interests


○ LLM Reasoning

Despite their impressive achievements across diverse domains, Large Language Models (LLMs) have struggled with logical reasoning. However, the recent development of even more advanced reasoning-capable models suggests that this aspect of LLMs has considerable untapped potential for further exploration and improvement.

Publications on this topic:

○ Reinforcement Learning

Reinforcement Learning (RL) has become a promising approach to solving many real-world problems, and the development and application of Deep RL algorithms are yet to be fully explored.
